SCHOOL TOURS: WHAT’S HAPPENING IN KANSAS CITY’S SCHOOLS?

See for yourself! Hop on the bus with other community members who are interested in learning about Kansas City’s numerous and varied school options. Together we’ll visit four different schools and explore ways to impact Kansas City students long after the tours are over. Tours are free, but registration, which you’ll find below, is required. All Community Members Welcome!



There are no tours scheduled right now..




A TYPICAL TOUR SCHEDULE

8:00 a.m. – Meet for breakfast and introductions 8:30 a.m. – Hop on the bus 8:40 a.m. to 12:50 p.m. – Tours of four schools, a combination of KCPS and charter schools 1:00 p.m. – Return to meeting spot for lunch and a conversation with school leaders 2:00 p.m. – Goodbye! Please plan to join us for the whole program from 8:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m. Sign-ups are first come, first served. Lunch is included. Only 30 spaces are available per tour and there is no fee to participate!
